The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (B.Sc. C.S.) program is designed to equip students with specialized knowledge and technological skills in computer science and its applications. This undergraduate program provides a strong foundation in programming, software development, database management, networking, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the dynamic IT industry. Established in 2001, the Department of Information Technology and Computer Science has been committed to nurturing young talent by providing quality education aligned with global industry requirements. The department emphasizes practical learning, innovation, and research, helping students develop problem-solving skills and a deep understanding of cutting-edge technologies. The program offers a comprehensive curriculum covering core subjects such as algorithms, data structures, cloud computing, and web technologies, along with hands-on learning through coding labs, industry projects, and internships, ensuring graduates are equipped with the skills required to excel in the ever-evolving technology landscape.

Program Highlights

  • University of Mumbai Affiliation: Industry-focused curriculum aligned with global IT careers under the University of Mumbai. Provides a strong foundation in theoretical and practical learning.
  • Industry Ties: Collaborations with Google, Zensor, and Oracle offer internships, certifications, and expert workshops. Enhances technical skills and career prospects.
  • Student Support: Mentorship, exchange programs, and career counseling help students excel. Skill-building sessions and hackathons keep them industry-ready.
  • Practical Training: Hands-on projects and coding labs prepare students for real-world challenges. Includes MH-CET (MCA) preparation for higher studies.
  • Alumni Network: A strong alumni network offers career guidance and placement support. Graduates mentor students and share industry insights.

Our Legacy

Established in 2001, the Department of Information Technology and Computer Science has been a hub for aspiring tech professionals, fostering innovation and excellence. We believe in a holistic approach to education, combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience through industry collaborations, research opportunities, and practical projects. Our curriculum is designed to equip students with cutting-edge skills in programming, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and data science, ensuring they are industry-ready and prepared for global career opportunities.
